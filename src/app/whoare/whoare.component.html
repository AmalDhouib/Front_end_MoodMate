About MoodMate

Your trusted AI companion for relaxation, emotional support, and mental wellness.

Our Mission

At MoodMate, we strive to make emotional well-being accessible to all. Whether you're interacting through text or voice, our chatbot listens, responds, and helps you manage your stress, anxiety, or simply accompany you during your moments of solitude.

Our mission is to help you feel better, every day, while tracking your emotions and offering real-time advice adapted to your mood.

Our Story

Started by a team of passionate developers and mental health advocates, MoodMate was born from the idea that technology should offer comfort. From basic chats to advanced emotion tracking, MoodMate has grown into a friendly assistant. It offers voice and text chat, provides tailored advice, and helps users monitor and understand their emotions through a personalized dashboard.
