About MoodMate

MoodMate is an intelligent chatbot designed to support you through writing, reading, and voice assistance. It offers personalized advice at every stage of your journey. You can explore interactive stories, track your emotional state daily, and review your weekly mood history. Every week, a new emotional report is generated to help you reflect and grow. MoodMate is here to make sure you feel heard, guided, and empowered every step of the way.
